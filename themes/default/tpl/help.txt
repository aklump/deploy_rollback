usage: deploy <command> [<args>]

These are your available commands, type 'deploy help <command>' for more info:

  status      See the status of your environment.
  offline     Take your website offline for testing.
  online      Bring your website online for normal traffic.
  pull        Deploy the project.
  rollback    Rollback to the state before last `pull` command.
  clean       Remove rollback data to prevent a rollback.

Calling 'deploy' without a command is equivalent to 'deploy help'
